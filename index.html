Hi, I’m Jingyang

Master student @ Model Pruning for Edge Scenarios

I am Jingyang Xiang (项靖阳), a Master Student in Advanced Perception on Robotics and Intelligent Learning Lab (APRIL) of Institute of Cyber-Systems and Control in College of Control Science and Engineering, Zhejiang University (ZJU). I am pursuing my M.S. degree in Zhejiang University in 2022, under supervision of Prof. Zaisheng Pan and Prof. Yong Liu. Before that, I obtained B.S. in Electrical Engineering and Automation from Zhejiang University of Technology in 2022, under supervision of Prof. Qi Xuan. My research works are focused on building efficient network for edge scenarios, e.g., deep learning, machine learning, model pruning, model distllation and model quantization.
